# Sales Dashboard
This dashboard provides an in-depth analysis of sales data for a given period, showcasing various metrics and visualizations. It covers the following key areas:

Brand and Product Overview: Displays the total number of brands (11), manufacturers (11), products (2.516K), categories (8), and sub-categories (32), with total sales of $37M and a profit of $22M.

Sales by Year and Quarter: A line chart that visualizes sales trends over three years (2021-2023) broken down by quarter, with notable fluctuations in quarterly sales amounts.

Sales by Country: A bar chart showing the distribution of sales across countries (United States, Australia, Canada, France, Germany, and Great Britain) over three years.

Sales by Class: Pie chart representing the percentage breakdown of sales by product class, with a focus on "Regular," "Deluxe," and "Economy" classes.

Sales by Brand: A treemap representing sales by individual brands, highlighting the contributions of key players like Contoso, Fabrikam, and Wide World Importers.

Sales by Category: A breakdown of sales by product category, including Computers ($13M), Home Appliances ($10M), and TV/Video ($7M), along with other categories like Cameras, Audio, and Music.

Monthly Sales Analysis: A table showing the monthly sales data, including sales amount, month-over-month (MoM) percentage change, and other relevant insights.
